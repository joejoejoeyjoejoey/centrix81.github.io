🔢 Algebra Fundamentals

What is Algebra?

Algebra uses letters (called variables) to represent unknown numbers. It helps us solve problems and find patterns!

Think of it this way:

If you have 3 boxes of cookies and each box has x cookies, you have 3x cookies total!

🎯 Understanding Probability

Probability measures how likely an event is to happen, from 0 (impossible) to 1 (certain).

Probability = Favorable outcomes ÷ Total possible outcomes

🎲 Example: Rolling a Die

What's the probability of rolling a 3?

  • Favorable outcomes: 1 (just the number 3)
  • Total outcomes: 6 (numbers 1-6)
  • Probability = 1/6 ≈ 0.167 or about 16.7%
